Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

We take your privacy seriously. Here's what we do with your information.

What Information We Collect

Activation Information

  • Email address (for activation)
  • Device identifier (hardware fingerprint)
  • Operating system version
  • License type (Trial or Full)

Usage Information

  • File format types processed
  • Approximate number of files scanned
  • General operation type
  • Session duration

Support Information

  • Your email address
  • Support request details
  • Error messages and logs (without file content)

What We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ Your documents or file contents
  • ❌ The words you're replacing
  • ❌ Sensitive personal data (passwords, credit cards, IDs)
  • ❌ Your personal behavior tracking
  • ❌ Government identification information

Your documents are processed entirely on YOUR computer.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data for:

  • ✓ License verification and management
  • ✓ Preventing unauthorized use
  • ✓ Providing support and assistance
  • ✓ Sending important updates
  • ✓ Improving software performance

We DO NOT use your data for:

  • ✗ Marketing or advertising
  • ✗ Selling to third parties
  • ✗ Tracking your behavior
  • ✗ Profiling or discrimination

Data Security

How We Protect Your Data:

  • All data transmitted via encrypted TLS/SSL
  • Sensitive data encrypted at rest on servers
  • Only authorized personnel can access data
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Intrusion detection systems in place
  • Regular data backups for disaster recovery

Your documents: Processed entirely on your computer. Never uploaded to our servers.

Your Privacy Rights

  • Right to Access: Request all data we hold
  • Right to Correction: Fix inaccurate data
  • Right to Deletion: Request data deletion
  • Right to Portability: Export your data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Opt-out anytime
  • Right to File Complaint: Contact data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, email team.docsie@gmail.com

Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period
Activation Data Duration of license
Support Records 12 months after resolution
Usage Analytics 12 months (anonymized)
Error Logs 30 days

GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you're in the EU or UK, you have full GDPR rights:

  • All data is handled according to GDPR standards
  • Your rights under GDPR are fully respected
  • Legal basis for processing documented
  • Data Protection Officer available for inquiries

CCPA Compliance (California Users)

If you're in California, you have these additional rights:

  • Right to know what data we collect
  • Right to delete your personal data
  • Right to opt-out of data sale (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising rights

Third-Party Services

We work with limited third parties:

  • Gumroad (Payment): Handles full version purchases. Subject to their privacy policy.
  • Email Service: Sends activation codes and support responses. Subject to their privacy policy.
  • Analytics: Basic anonymized usage statistics only. No personal tracking.

Contact & Support

Questions about your privacy? Contact us:

team.docsie@gmail.com

Subject: "Privacy Inquiry"

Response time: 24-48 hours

🔒 Privacy Promise

We promise to:

  • Never sell your personal information
  • Never share your email with marketers
  • Never track your document processing
  • Never store your documents on our servers
  • Always be transparent about data practices
  • Respect your privacy rights fully
