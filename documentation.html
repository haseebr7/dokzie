Features

Bulk Word Replacer

Find and replace words across multiple files simultaneously (.docx, .xlsx, .pptx, .pdf, .txt, .csv). Includes:

  • 3,000+ Files/Min — Tested and verified performance on large directories
  • Live Preview — See all changes file-by-file before applying
  • Similarity Threshold Slider — Adjustable 10%–100% to catch typos and spelling variants
  • Whole Word Mode — Prevents partial word matches (ON by default)

Smart Word Finder

A read-only scanner that finds words across all your documents without modifying anything. Dokzie includes:

  • Deep Scan — Recursively scans subfolders (Word Finder only)
  • Similarity Threshold Slider — Finds similar words and spelling variants
  • Whole Word Matching — Option to include or exclude substrings
  • Clean Results — Only shows files with matches, with exact counts and percentages

Fuzzy Matching (Both Features)

Intelligent similarity detection available in both Replacer and Finder. Catches typos like "Centre" vs "Center" and "Johanson" vs "Johansson" automatically.

Backup (Replacer Only)

Automatically saves pristine copies of original files before making changes. Your data is always safe — you can always revert.

Troubleshooting

❓ Files Not Showing in Folder

Ensure folder contains supported file formats. Check file permissions and try a different folder.

❓ Slow Processing Speed

Close unnecessary applications, reduce folder size, or disable Fuzzy Matching for faster processing.

❓ Changes Not Applied

Ensure files aren't open in other applications. Try running as Administrator and check disk space.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my data secure?

A: Yes. Files are processed 100% locally on your computer. Nothing is uploaded, transmitted, or stored on any server. Core features work completely offline.

Q: Does my data ever leave my computer?

A: No. Dokzie processes everything locally. The only internet connection used is for license activation/verification — your actual documents never touch the network.

Q: What does the Similarity Threshold do?

A: It controls how closely a word must match your search term. At 100%, only exact matches are found. At 85% (default), common variants and typos like "Centre" vs "Center" are caught. Lower it further to find more distant matches.

Q: What is Deep Scan?

A: Deep Scan (available in Word Finder) recursively searches through all subfolders of your selected directory. Without it, only files in the top-level folder are scanned.

Q: Can I use Dokzie on multiple computers?

A: Your activation code works on one computer at a time. Contact us for multi-computer licensing.

Q: What if I lose my activation code?

A: Email us with your registered email and we'll resend it.
